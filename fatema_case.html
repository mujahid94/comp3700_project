Fatema`s Case

Case Title: Property Dispute Resolution

Court: Muscat Civil Court

Case Number: 2024/3490

Judge: Hon. Abdullah Al-Harthy

Case Details:

Fatema filed a lawsuit against his neighbor regarding a property boundary dispute. The issue escalated when unauthorized construction was carried out on the disputed land. The plaintiff sought a resolution and compensation for the encroachment.

Court Proceedings:

The court examined the property deeds, heard testimonies from both parties, and reviewed evidence, including a land survey report. Mediation efforts were made but failed.

Judgment:

The court ruled in favor of Salim, ordering the defendant to cease construction, pay a compensation amount of OMR 15,000, and cover court costs.

Next Steps:

  • Follow-up on compensation payment.
  • Ensure the construction halt is enforced.
